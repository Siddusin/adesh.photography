About Me

Born in Aland, raised in Kalaburagi, and forever consumed with answering the question: Why is that compelling? Taking pictures has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember.


I am available for private portrait sessions, as well as events in the Banglore and in the North Indian regions -- though I would also be very interested in working on location elsewhere.


I'd love to hear from you, so feel free to contact me.
